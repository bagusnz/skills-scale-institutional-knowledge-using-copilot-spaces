# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads define and execute quality assurance strategies, manage testing efforts, and ensure products meet acceptance criteria and quality standards before release.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain test plans aligned with features and acceptance criteria
- Build and execute manual and automated test suites
- Identify, document, and prioritize defects
- Collaborate with developers to understand technical design and edge cases
- Validate acceptance criteria before features move to Done
- Mentor team on testing best practices and tools

### Goals
- Maximize product quality and customer satisfaction
- Reduce production defects and rework
- Enable fast, confident releases with comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Test status reports and defect tracking
- Collaboration with developers on test design and coverage
- QA review gates before release

---

## Technical Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Architects define system design, technology choices, and long-term technical direction. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with organizational standards.

### Responsibilities
- Lead technical design reviews and architecture decisions
- Advise on technology selection and trade-offs
- Ensure alignment with existing systems and standards
- Mentor developers on architectural patterns and best practices
- Identify and mitigate technical risks
- Collaborate with product to balance features with technical sustainability

### Goals
- Build scalable, maintainable systems
- Reduce technical debt and improve system health
- Enable fast, sustainable feature delivery

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Architecture decision records (ADRs)
- Code review feedback on architectural compliance
- Regular sync with engineering leadership

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove process impediments, and coach teams on continuous improvement. They enable team flow and protect the team from disruptions.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, planning, retrospectives, and reviews
- Identify and help resolve process blockers and impediments
- Coach team on agile practices and ceremonies
- Track and communicate team metrics (velocity, burndown)
- Support continuous improvement initiatives
- Shield team from external interruptions during sprints

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Build a high-performing, self-organizing team
- Enable consistent, predictable delivery

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of all agile ceremonies
- Impediment logs and process improvement tracking
- Metrics reporting and retrospective action items
- One-on-ones with team members

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts translate business requirements into clear, actionable specifications. They bridge business stakeholders and technical teams to ensure solutions deliver business value.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and clarify business requirements from stakeholders
- Create detailed requirements specifications and user stories
- Define acceptance criteria aligned with business outcomes
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs
- Identify process improvements and optimization opportunities
- Document and communicate requirements to technical teams

### Goals
- Deliver solutions that address real business needs
- Reduce rework due to misunderstood requirements
- Enable faster time-to-value

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Requirements documentation and user story refinement
- Acceptance criteria validation
- Backlog refinement sessions

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Security Champions advocate for security practices, threat awareness, and secure development. They integrate security considerations into every phase of project delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs and code for security risks
- Lead threat modeling sessions for new features
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Coordinate security testing and vulnerability scans
- Educate team on secure development practices
- Escalate security incidents and coordinate response

### Goals
- Build security into products from the start
- Reduce security vulnerabilities and compliance risk
- Foster a security-aware culture

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews of designs and PRs
- Security incident response and post-mortems
- Training and awareness communications
- Compliance and audit reporting

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When assigning tasks or running scenarios, consider the interactions and dependencies between roles to model realistic project dynamics.
